What I've Built

A look at the products, platforms, and problems I've tackled through code.

CondoHub

CondoHub is a smart management platform designed to simplify life in residential buildings. From streamlined communication between residents and managers to automated billing, maintenance tracking, and facility bookings—this system brings efficiency, transparency, and peace of mind to condo living.

Shipperly

Shipperly is a transportation management system that automates freight pricing for e-commerce platforms. By integrating with multiple carriers via API, it instantly retrieves real-time shipping rates based on package data, destination, and delivery preferences—empowering stores to offer the best delivery options at checkout. It reduces manual quoting time to zero and has helped businesses cut shipping costs by up to 25% while improving conversion rates through transparent pricing.

SkillBridge

SkillBridge is a platform that connects professionals with people seeking expert advice—anytime, anywhere. Whether it's business strategy, legal guidance, career coaching, or tech help, users can browse verified consultants, book sessions, and meet remotely through a seamless interface. It empowers professionals to monetize their knowledge, and makes expert advice more accessible to everyone. Trusted by consultants across multiple industries, SkillBridge is redefining how people connect to expertise.
